History of Monte Carlo

Monte Carlo is a district of the Principality of Monaco, located on the French Riviera. It is famous for its luxury casinos, glamourous hotels, and beautiful coastline.

The district was founded in the 19th century as a resort for wealthy Europeans seeking a winter retreat. The first casino, the Casino de Monte-Carlo, was built in 1863 and is still a major attraction today.

Monte Carlo has also been the site of many historic events, including the marriage of Prince Rainier III and Grace Kelly in 1956.

Events in Monte Carlo

Monte Carlo Rally

The Monte Carlo Rally is an annual rally race held in January. It is one of the oldest and most prestigious rallies in the world, and attracts drivers and spectators from around the globe.

Monte Carlo Rolex Masters

The Monte Carlo Rolex Masters is an annual tennis tournament held in April. It is part of the ATP World Tour Masters 1000, and features some of the top male tennis players in the world.
